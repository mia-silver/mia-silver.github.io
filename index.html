CSCE 190: Mia Silver

Mia Silver's CSCE 190 Site

Problem Statement: Affordable Event Finder

People miss out on local events since they struggle to find information on events occurring in their city that are affordable.

Affinity Diagram: Brainstorming

We made categories for events, who will use/benefit from the app, things that influence the problem, and different logistics involved with the app.

Persona List: 5 Typical Users

We made different persona's representing typical users for our Event Finder varying in age, job, city, and mariage status, etc.

Storyboards: How Will People Use our App

We all made storyboards for our respective personas about them using the app in one way or another.

Sketch: What our App Will Look Like

We all made relatively similar designs for our event finder app, each having at least a list of events, a menu, a search function.
